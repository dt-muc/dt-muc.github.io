Crash course „Design Thinking your life“

We all know Design Thinking as methodology and mindset at work. In a team oriented and user centric way we use the process to solve our users problems with innovative solutions.

Interestingly the same methodology can be applied to personal challenges. You don’ t believe it? Then join us for this after-work design thinking crash course on „Design Thinking your life“ and find it out.

With the help of our coaches, you’ll work together in teams of two to find your own little design challenge. Together with your vis-a-vis you will work on creative ideas and built an action plan to bring the solution into your life.

After the crash course, you will also have the possibility to see and experience some of our innovation showcases. We build prototypes with Design Thinking, SAP software and new technologies (such as AR, VR, ML) to inspire our development teams and engage with our customers.

The SAP Munich Design Thinkers are looking forward to spend a fun evening with you in our own Labs and Innovation space. See you there!

PEOPLE

Your hosts are the Design Thinking coaches from SAP in Munich, namely Agnieszka, Alice, Denise, Lilit, Sebastian and Yevgeny. We are all having different job roles, like agile coach, scrum master, developer, presales expert, storyteller and designer. And we are all united under the mission to bring design thinking to daily (work) life.

Hello, World!

Das Ziel der Design Thinking München Gruppe ist es, Menschen, die sich mit der Methode "Design Thinking" auseinandersetzen wollen, in und um München regelmäßig zusammenzubringen, und einen persönlichen Erfahrungsaustausch zu etablieren. Dazu dienen neben den Xing-Gruppen-Möglichkeiten insbesondere der persönliche Erfahrungsaustausch, organisiert in informellen Treffen, Diskussion von Erfahrungsberichten und Case Studies bis hin zu Vorträge und Workshops. Diese werden auch über Twitter @dt_muc und Facebook bekannt gegeben. Detaillierte Information über vergangene Veranstaltungen findet ihr in unserem Wiki.
